DESCRIPTION

Edgar Daniel Nixon was a civil rights activist. who was alive from 1899 to 1987. E.D. Nixon had a profession as a Pullman porter, and a community activist. He had leadership positions in the NAACP and help bail Rosa Parks out of jail for the bus incident. He also recruited Dr. Martin Luther King Jr.E.D.
